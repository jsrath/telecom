How can you get Telecom Passes?

If you're not a Telecom customer yet

Pick one of our new plans and enjoy the unlimited social experience! In your webshop basket, you can easily add further passes to your subscription.

If you're already a Telecom customer

In the MyTelecom App, you can easily add any Telecom Pass to your existing subscription. Check the list of plans compatible with the Passes.

If you need further information

You can call 1401 on any weekday from 8 AM to 8 PM free of charge from any domestic network, or visit your nearest Telecom store. You can also find some answers quickly in the FAQ as well.

Good to know

We will take care about your Telecom Pass data traffic

The applications, included in the Telecom Passes, do not consumer your data. Thus if you are uploading a photo to Instagram, video chatting on Messenger, listening music on Spotify or watching video on Youtube, your data amount will not decrease.

You only need to have some minimal general data left to use your Telecom Passes

This may be your standard data included in your subscription, or any other Plusz Adat option. It is important to have some minimal data left on your plan to keep Passes active. In case of your new subscription plans, if the entirety of your data runs out, the Telecom Passes will be provided with an up/download speed of 0/0 Mbps. Purchase additional data (Plusz Adat) in the MyTelecom App, and the apps included in your Telecom Pass package can be used again without consuming your mobile data.

Be careful with embedded content

Embedded content is still considered as regular data traffic, as it is no longer the bundled app's own content. This means that your general mobile data will be used to access it.

Continuously updated content

Telecom Passes are an open platform for any thematic service provider who would like to be included in one of the bundles.

Further Details

Important info about the Video and Music Passes

Video Pass provides a 480p video resolution, which is optimized to the mobile handset available at the launch of the promotion. This option does not include access to the premium features of a video or music service provided by a third party for a monthly fee. For this paid content, a separate subscription is required by the video and music provider.

The Telecom Pass experience follows you anywhere in the EU

You can use your Telecom Passes in the EU the same way as in Hungary, as long as you have a data allowance that can be used free of charge in the EU, and you have some data left on that plan.

Not all the services of the applications are for free of charge

The applications itself do not consume your data, however Telecom subscriptions do not apply for paid services included in the applications, so they can be purchased directly from the operator of the application. (e.g. purchasing stickers through Viber or watching premium content at Netflix)

Updating your apps uses your regular mobil data

Since the apps included in Telecom Passes are not updated within the apps themselves, but through the GooglePlay or AppStore apps, updating your apps will be considered as regular mobile data usage.

Pass service is valid for personal usage and is not shareable

Telecom Pass is for personal data usage, it can not be shared. To access Telecom Pass, it requires downloading the mobile application of the given provider and the appropriate device and APN setting. Telecom only provides internet access to the application and has no effect on the specific application and the usage conditions defined by each service provider. Separation of traffic types by Telecom Pass is approximately, but not guaranteed 100% accuracy.
